Government of Yukon to attend Association of Yukon Communities annual general meeting

Published May 10, 2018

Premier Silver will be joined by Ministers Streicker, Mostyn and Dendys in Dawson City this weekend for the Association of Yukon Communities’ annual general meeting.

The meeting of municipalities provides an opportunity to connect with municipal leaders and advance discussion on key issues, including solid waste, infrastructure and cannabis legalization.

This year’s meeting runs from May 10 to 13.

The Association of Yukon Communities continues to be a valuable partner for addressing vital community issues. We are committed to building and maintaining respectful, collaborative working relationships with municipalities and we look forward to the many important discussions that will occur this weekend.

Minister of Community Services John Streicker
Quick facts

  • The Association of Yukon Communities was founded in 1974 with a focus on establishing responsible government at the community level and providing a united approach to community ambitions.

  • This is the 43rd annual general meeting of the Association of Yukon Communities.
