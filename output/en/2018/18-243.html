Yukon entrepreneurs profiled in new video series

Published November 14, 2018

Yukon entrepreneurs are showcased in a new series of documentary-style videos commissioned by the Government of Yukon. The series profiles eight Yukon businesses on their journey through entrepreneurship. The videos highlight the territory’s growing entrepreneurial workforce as well as the opportunities and advantages available to those looking to start a business in Yukon.

The videos will premiere at an event in Whitehorse on November 14 at the North of Ordinary Experience Centre from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m.

Entrepreneurs enrich our communities while contributing to the growth and diversification of Yukon’s economy, and it’s important that we support them and highlight their successes. These videos showcase young Yukoners, their strong entrepreneurial spirit and the variety of opportunities that make Yukon a place where anyone can work hard to build a great future.

Minister of Economic Development Ranj Pillai
Quick facts

  • The North of Ordinary Experience Centre is located at 4201 4th Avenue, Whitehorse.

  • Yukon entrepreneurs profiled in the series: GBP Creative, BulletHole Bagels, Bella Elite Events, 14 Acre Farm, Husky Bus/The Klondike Experience, Winterlong Brewing, Trinity Technology and CG Contracting.

  • Filming took place in Dawson City, Teslin, Whitehorse and Haines Junction.

  • The video series is 100 per cent Yukon-made, with filming, editing, music and video provided by White Hole Productions, Jordy Walker and Outpost 31 Media Inc.
