The Government of Yukon and the Yukon Association of Education Professionals reach tentative agreement

Published December 7, 2021

The Government of Yukon and the Yukon Association of Education Professionals, formerly known as the Yukon Teachers’ Association (YTA), have reached a tentative agreement in collective bargaining on December 2, 2021.

The agreement will be submitted for ratification by the respective parties. No details will be released until the agreement is ratified.

Quick facts

  • The Yukon Teachers’ Association announced a name change today and is now known as the Yukon Association of Education Professionals.

  • The current collective agreement expired on June 30, 2021.

  • Members of the Yukon Association of Education Professionals must now vote to ratify the new collective agreement.

  • The Yukon Association of Education Professionals represents educational professionals in Yukon schools and has 1,119 current active members.
