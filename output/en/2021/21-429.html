October 28, 2021: public exposure notifications

Please note that the flight details for Flight 4N761 have been updated.

Monday, October 25, 2021
Whitehorse to Vancouver

Air North, Flight 4N563

Saturday, October 23, 2021

Calgary to Edmonton to Whitehorse
Calgary departure: 7:50 p.m., arrival 8:40 p.m.
Edmonton departure: 9:40 p.m., arrival 11:30 p.m.

Canada Games Centre, Wellness Centre and hot tub area

Tuesday, October 26, 2021
Between 8 p.m. and 10:30 p.m.

Education notifications

Grow with Joy Child Care, Unit E ‒ 2157 Second Avenue, Whitehorse

Class: infant groups
Dates: Monday, October 18, to Wednesday, October 20
Who is affected: staff and children in the infant groups

Yukon Communicable Disease Control has contacted everyone directly through notification letters.

Testing and vaccines

If any symptoms appear, no matter how mild, self-isolate, remain at home and arrange to get tested either by calling 867-393-3083 or booking a test online. Rural Yukon residents should contact their community health centre to arrange for testing.
